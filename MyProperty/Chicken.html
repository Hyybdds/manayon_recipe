Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream, stirring continuously. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir until the sauce thickens.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Toss the cooked fettuccine in the Alfredo sauce until well coated.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley before serving.
