Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 300g spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, saut√© the chopped onion until golden brown.
  2. Add the minced garlic and ground beef, and cook until the beef is browned.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, red wine (if using), dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and toss with the Bolognese sauce.
  5. Serve the spaghetti Bolognese topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
